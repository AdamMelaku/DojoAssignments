What are Databases?

A structured set of data


Databases are a collection of information, they are used for creating, manipulating, and deleting data. There are many different types of databases and they can be classified in a basic approach of relational database or a non-relational database. Relationships means a set of our data is related to another section of our data. Meanwhile in a non-databases are put into secions of data called schemas, inside each schema each rows to house that piticular set of data.
